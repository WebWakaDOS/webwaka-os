## Purpose

White-labeling is a first-class capability allowing approved entities to present WebWaka-powered experiences under their own branding.

## Scope

White-label capability may apply to:

- Partner admin surfaces
- Brand surfaces
- Discovery-derived microsites
- Downstream partner experiences

## Rules

1. White-label rights are subscription-controlled and cannot be self-activated outside of a qualifying plan.
2. Brand surfaces and discovery profiles are distinct — discovery profiles remain WebWaka-branded unless explicitly separated.
3. Sub-white-labeling (a Partner granting white-label to a Sub-Partner) must be explicitly permitted by the Partner's entitlement.
4. Shared platform governance and compliance requirements still apply to all experiences even when they appear under partner branding.
5. WebWaka attribution rules are defined per plan tier and may not be removed below the minimum required attribution level.
